Company Overview

At MTI Instruments, we design, manufacture and sell precision non-contact physical measurement solutions, condition based monitoring systems, portable balancing equipment and semiconductor wafer inspection tools.

At MTI Instruments, we offer a comprehensive array of products that are equipped with technologies that solve complex real world applications in numerous industries. When it comes to electronic gauging instruments for position, displacement and vibration applications, we have the right tools for you. Our technologies solve measurement applications ranging from capacitance to laser and fiber-optic sensors, which can provide resolution up to 0.01 uin. (2.5 Angstroms).
